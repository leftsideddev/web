# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: March 24, 2026*

---

## 1. Overview

Left-Sided Studios ("Left-Sided Studios," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website or use any related digital services we operate (collectively, the "Services").

As an independent studio, we prioritize transparency and minimal data collection. Our Services are designed to function without requiring user accounts, and we collect only the information strictly necessary to operate, maintain, and improve what we offer. We believe your data is yours — we are only temporary stewards of what you choose to share with us.

By accessing or using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services.

## 2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected:

- Through our website and any subdomains operated by Left-Sided Studios

This policy **does not apply** to third-party websites, platforms, or services that may be linked from our Services. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external services you interact with.

## 3. Information We Collect

We aim to collect as little information as possible. The following sections describe the categories of information we may collect, the circumstances in which we collect it, and why.

---

### 3.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

You may choose to share information with us when you:

- Contact us via email
- Submit a message through a contact form on our website
- Communicate with us for support, feedback, or business inquiries
- Reach out through our official email

This information may include:

| Data Type | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Email address** | If you contact us directly via email |
| **Name** | Only if you choose to include it in your message |

---

### 3.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, certain technical information is collected automatically by our hosting and delivery infrastructure. This is standard practice across the web and is necessary for basic site operation and security.

This information may include:

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **IP address** | Security monitoring, abuse prevention, and geographic analytics |
| **Browser type and version** | Compatibility and performance optimization |
| **Operating system** | Compatibility and performance optimization |
| **Referring/exit pages** | Understanding how users navigate to and from our site |
| **Date and time of access** | Security logging and traffic analysis |
| **Pages visited** | Understanding which content is most useful to visitors |

This data is collected and stored in server logs by our hosting provider, **Netlify**, and is used solely for the operational and security purposes described above. We do not use this information to identify or track individual users.

For more information on how Netlify handles data, please refer to [Netlify's Privacy Policy](https://www.netlify.com/privacy/).

---

### 3.3 Cookies and Local Storage

We use a minimal number of cookies or similar browser-based technologies, and only for functional purposes. Specifically:

- **Theme preference:** We store your preference for Dark Mode or Light Mode so that it persists between visits.

We do **not** use cookies or similar technologies for:

- Advertising or ad targeting
- Cross-site behavioral tracking
- User profiling or analytics sold to third parties
- Any purpose beyond basic site functionality

You can control, block, or delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect visual preference features such as theme persistence.

## 10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Right of Access** | Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you |
| **Right to Rectification** | Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information |
| **Right to Erasure** | Request deletion of your personal information ("right to be forgotten") |
| **Right to Restriction** | Request that we limit how we process your information |
| **Right to Object** | Object to certain types of processing, such as direct marketing |
| **Right to Portability** | Request that we provide your data in a portable, machine-readable format |

These rights may vary based on your jurisdiction. For example:

- **EU/EEA residents** may have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
- **California residents** may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
- **UK residents** may have rights under the UK GDPR

To exercise any applicable rights, please contact us using the information in Section 12. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe (generally within 30 days) and in accordance with applicable laws. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling certain requests.
